Our Mission

Homestead Robotics (FRC Team 670) strives to provide students at Homestead High School with a learning experience that develops technical background and problem solving skills through the process of building successful robots. We aim to inspire students, both on our team and throughout the community, to pursue their interests in STEM.

Teach

We would like to teach the next generation of potential engineers of basic electrical, mechanical, and computer engineering knowledge through our activities, workshops, and other robotics events.

Inspire

With our projects, robots, and other activities, Team 670 encourages all members to explore their interest in a variety of fields such as engineering, business, artistic design, and community service.

Problem Solve

Through our robotic seasons, members have the unique opportunity to learn the most basic skill for an engineer: problem solving. We provide a variety of challenges such as design problems and physics application.
