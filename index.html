FRC Team 670

Team 670 has participated in the FIRST Robotics competition since 2001. In recent years, the team enjoyed success during the Ultimate Ascent, Aerial Assist, and Recycle Rush games.

Silicon Valley Regional 2015

At this year's SVR competition, Team 670 ranked 6th out of () teams from across the country and advanced to semi-finals as the 4th alliance captain.

Central Valley Regional 2015

This regional was the first one of the year for Team 670. At Madera South High School, we ranked 8th and played in the quarter-finals in the 5th alliance.

CalGames 2014

At CalGames 2014 hosted by Woodside High school, the team competed with teams all around the San Francisco Bay area, It was a great way to introduce new members to the club.

Off Season Events

During the off season, HRT engages team members and other students to participate in numerous events to prepare for the build season. The annual inner-school VEX competition, Operation Revive Old Robot, and workshops are some activites we do.

Community Services

Homestead Robotics is heavily involved with the community. HRT has participated in showcases, presentations, and other volunteer work to reach out to the public and spread knowledge about STEM.

Who Are We?

We are a high school robotics team based in Cupertino, California that is dedicated to spread knowledge and encourage interest in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Maths).Every year, Homestead Robotics competes in the international FIRST Robotics Competition as Team 670.

Our Sponsors

Team 670 is funded by generous sponsors. We welcome donations of all amounts. For more information, visit our Sponsors page.

Have a question or comment?
